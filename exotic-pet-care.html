The Problem

Every species deserves smart health tracking

Every pet health app was built for dogs and cats. If you keep reptiles, fish, or birds, you're stuck with apps that don't understand your animals.

🚫

No species-specific tracking

Generic apps don't track water parameters, shedding cycles, humidity levels, or egg production. They just give you a text field.

📱

Juggling multiple apps

One app for your snake's feeding log, another for your fish tank's water tests, a spreadsheet for your chickens. Nothing talks to each other.

🤖

AI that doesn't get it

Try asking an AI chatbot about your bearded dragon's health — without context about their basking temps, shedding history, or feeding schedule.

📋

Manual everything

No app scans your exotic vet's prescriptions or records your vet visits. You're typing everything by hand, if you track it at all.

The Solution

One app for all your pets. Finally.

VetGPT combines species-specific tracking with AI intelligence — no other app does both.

🌡️

Species-Specific Tracking

Water parameters for fish, shedding logs for reptiles, egg production for poultry, humidity for amphibians — real tracking, not text fields.

📄

Scan Vet Documents

Photograph prescriptions, vaccine records, and lab results. AI extracts medication names, dosages, and dates automatically.

💬

AI That Knows Your Pets

Ask anything about your bearded dragon's health. The AI knows their husbandry data, vet history, medications, and species needs.

🏠

Enclosure Management

Track aquariums, terrariums, cages, and coops. Link multiple pets to shared enclosures. Log parameters per habitat.

📊

AI Health Scores

A 0–100 score per pet based on care compliance, vet visits, and health patterns — with specific actions to improve it.

📸

Health Journey

Weekly photos to track changes over time. AI analyzes appearance and provides species-aware observations.
