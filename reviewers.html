Feature Walkthrough

  1. Sign in using the demo credentials above. You'll land on the Home screen showing the pet carousel, daily wellness tips, and quick actions.
  2. Swipe through pet cards on the home screen to see different pets (dog, cat, fish). Each has a Health Score and species-specific tip.
  3. Tap "AI Health Dashboard" on any pet's overview to see the AI-generated Health Score breakdown, recommended actions, health strengths, and areas to watch.
  4. Open "AI Chat" from the bottom navigation. Select a pet, then ask a question like "What health issues should I watch for?" The AI responds with context about that specific pet.
  5. Test document scanning: In AI Chat, tap the attachment icon and upload or photograph a prescription label or vet document. The AI will extract and present the key information.
  6. Visit the Health tab to see the logging interface — Symptoms, Meds, Inventory, Visits, and species-specific tabs (e.g., "Water" for fish).
  7. Check the Pets tab to see all pet profiles, digital wallet (vaccine/insurance cards), pet journey (weekly photos), and medical records.
  8. Test reminders: Tap "Reminder" from Quick Actions on the home screen to create a test reminder. Notifications will fire at the configured time.

AI Disclosure

VetGPT uses third-party AI services to power its intelligent features:

  • Text and vision AI — Used for document scanning, photo analysis, health chat, health scores, and wellness tips
  • Speech-to-text AI — Used for vet visit voice recording transcription

All AI features include clear disclaimers that they do not constitute veterinary advice and that users should consult a licensed veterinarian for medical decisions. The AI is positioned as an informational tool, not a diagnostic one.

Permissions Used

  • Camera: For scanning prescriptions, vaccine cards, documents, and taking pet photos
  • Photo Library: For uploading existing photos and documents
  • Microphone: For voice recording of vet visits
  • Notifications: For medication reminders, appointment alerts, and wellness tips
