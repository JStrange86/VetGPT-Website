Delete Your Data

Last updated: February 15, 2026

VetGPT gives you full control over your data. You can delete specific information without deleting your entire account. Here's how to manage and delete different types of data.

Keep Your Account: Deleting data from these sections removes only that specific information. Your account and other data remain intact.

Types of Data You Can Delete

🐕 Pet Profiles

Remove a pet and all their associated health records, vet visits, and medications.

Pets Tab → Select Pet → Edit → Delete Pet

📋 Health Logs

Delete individual symptom entries, observations, or health notes.

Health Tab → Health Logs → Swipe left to delete

🏥 Vet Visits

Remove vet visit records including diagnoses, treatments, and notes.

Health Tab → Visits → Select visit → Delete

💊 Medications

Delete medication entries and their tracking history.

Health Tab → Medications → Swipe left to delete

💉 Vaccinations

Remove vaccination records from your pet's profile.

Health Tab → Vaccines → Swipe left to delete

📄 Documents & Photos

Delete uploaded documents, scanned records, and photos.

Profile → Medical Records → Select → Delete

🎙️ Voice Recordings

Remove vet visit recordings and their AI transcriptions.

Profile → Audio Recordings → Select → Delete

💬 Chat History

Clear your AI conversation history.

AI Chat Tab → Settings icon → Clear History

Delete All Data

If you want to delete all your data and your account entirely, see our account deletion page.

Need Help?

If you need assistance deleting specific data or have questions about your information, contact us at support@vetgpt.app.
