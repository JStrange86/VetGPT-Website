THE VET JOURNEY

From first symptom to treatment plan

This is how VetGPT tracks your pet's health over time — not just snapshots, but the full story.

Week 1 You notice something's off Your dog starts drinking more water than usual. You log the symptom and start tracking daily intake. 📝 Symptom logged + photos
Week 3 First vet visit You record the appointment. AI transcribes the vet's notes and extracts blood work orders and follow-up instructions. 🎙️ Visit recorded + AI extracted
Week 5 Lab results come back You scan the lab report. AI pulls out abnormal values and explains what they mean in plain language — in the context of your pet's symptoms. 📄 Document scanned + analyzed
Week 6 Diagnosis and new medication Cushing's disease. New medication prescribed. You snap a photo of the bottle — VetGPT extracts the name, dosage, frequency, and sets up reminders. 💊 Medication auto-tracked
Week 8+ Monitoring progress Weekly photos show visible improvement. Water intake normalizes. You open AI chat and ask "how is she responding to treatment?" — and it knows everything. 📈 AI tracks progress over time

Scan Anything

Record Vet Visits
