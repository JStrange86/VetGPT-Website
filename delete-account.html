Delete Your Account

Last updated: February 15, 2026

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to permanently delete your VetGPT account and all associated data.

⚠️ This action is permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including pet profiles, health records, vet visits, medications, and any uploaded documents. This cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open VetGPT

Launch the VetGPT app on your iOS or Android device and sign in to your account.

2

Go to Profile

Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.

3

Scroll to Data & Privacy

Scroll down to find the Data & Privacy section.

4

Tap "Delete Account"

Tap the Delete Account button and confirm your decision when prompted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and account credentials
  • All pet profiles and their information
  • Health logs, symptoms, and observations
  • Vet visit records and notes
  • Vaccination records
  • Medication tracking data
  • Uploaded documents and photos
  • AI conversation history
  • Notification preferences and reminders

Deletion Timeline: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. During this period, your data is no longer accessible and cannot be recovered.

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account through the app, or if you have questions about your data, please contact us at support@vetgpt.app.

If you'd like to delete specific data without deleting your entire account, see our data deletion options.
